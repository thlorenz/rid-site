Sponsor

Rid is Sponsorware and thus not open sourced yet.

You can sponsor me via a monthly contribution and gain immediate access to the currently private rid repository.

I will fully open source rid to everyone once I reach 60 sponsors.

By sponsoring me you not only show your appreciation for all the work that went into rid already, but also help me evolve, stabilize and maintain it.

Sponsors also receive early access to screencasts and tutorials about rid.

I greatly appreciate it. 🙏 ❤️
